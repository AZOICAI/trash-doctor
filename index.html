Why Clean Your Bins?

Dirty bins can cause more problems than you think.

🦠

Bacteria Growth

Bins can harbor salmonella, E. coli and listeria.

🪰

Flies & Maggots

Rotting residue attracts flies that lay eggs in your bin.

👃

Bad Odors

Built-up grime causes smells you notice from the driveway.

🐀

Attracts Pests

Raccoons, rodents and insects love a dirty bin.

🧤

Unsightly Grime

Nobody wants to touch — or smell — a filthy trash can.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be home for my cleaning?

Nope! Just leave your bins at the curb after your trash pickup and we'll take care of the rest. You'll get a text when your bins are sparkling clean.

When do you clean my bins?

We schedule cleanings on the same day as your regular trash pickup, right after your bins are emptied.

Is the cleaning safe for kids and pets?

Yes. We use eco-friendly, biodegradable products that are completely safe for your family, pets, and lawn.

What if my bin still has trash in it?

We can only clean empty bins. If your bin isn't empty when we arrive, we'll text you to reschedule at no charge.

Can I cancel my plan anytime?

Absolutely. There are no contracts — pause or cancel your plan whenever you like.
